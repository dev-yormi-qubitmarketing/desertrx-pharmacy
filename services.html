Non-Narcotic Pain Solutions

Opioid or narcotic addiction is an epidemic that has lead to fatalities all over the country. Here at DesertRx Pharmacy we want to provide our customers pain relief in the safest way possible. With coordination through your primary healthcare provider we seek to provide non-narcotic pain solutions. Our pharmacy counselors can work with you to choose what best fits your condition. Our shelves are stocked with:

  • Topical pain relievers (such as medicinal creams and ointments).

  • Non-opioid prescription drugs (triptans used for headaches or migraines).

  • Over-the-counter medications (pills that don't require a prescription for mild to moderate pain).

Talk to your doctor first to find out the best course of action for your pain symptoms.
